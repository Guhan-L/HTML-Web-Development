About Rohan Private Library

Rohan Private Library is a dedicated learning space designed to support students, researchers, and knowledge seekers by providing a calm and well-organized learning environment. Inspired by modern library standards such as the Anna Centenary Library, the facility offers academic books, competitive examination materials, reference resources, and digital learning support.
