Types of AI

AI is generally categorized based on its capabilities:

Narrow AI (Weak AI): The only type that currently exists in practice. These systems are designed to perform a single or narrow range of tasks, such as facial recognition, internet searches, or acting as a virtual assistant(like Siri or Alexa). General AI (Strong AI): A theoretical type of AI with human-like cognitive capabilities, able to understand, learn, and apply its intelligence to solve any problem, just like a human would. This does not yet exist. Superintelligent AI: A hypothetical future AI that would surpass human intelligence across all fields, including creativity and general wisdom.
